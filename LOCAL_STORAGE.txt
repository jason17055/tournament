=item webtd.tournament.EID.roster

Contains a comma-delimited list of PIDs.

=item webtd.tournament.EID.plays

Comma-separated list of play IDs.

=item webtd.roster.PID.name

Contains a player's name. PID is a positive integer.

=item webtd.roster.PID.number

Contains the player's AGA number.

=item webtd.plays.ID.player1

The Player ID of White.

=item webtd.plays.ID.player2

The Player ID of Black.

=item webtd.plays.ID.winner

Either 'w' or 'b', indicating the winner.

=item webtd.plays.ID.handicapStones

The non-negative integer number of handicap Stones.

=item webtd.plays.ID.komi

The integer number of komi points given to White.
